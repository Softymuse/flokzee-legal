  • Other users: information you choose to make public (profile, content, events) according to your settings.
  • Authorities: when legally required or to protect rights and safety.
  • International transfers: if data leaves the EU/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses).

6. Retention

Subject to applicable law (including GDPR), you may request access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability, and object to processing. You may withdraw consent at any time and lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise your rights: christy.yesso@gmail.com.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No service is perfectly secure; please use a strong password and your device’s protections.

9. Children

Flokzee is for users aged 18 and over only. If we learn that a minor created an account, we will delete it and remove associated data.
